Sea Frieght

CMS delivers highly flexible seafreight services through partnerships with leading carriers and tailor-made rates. With 18 years of experience in the industry, we have built solid cooperation with our worldwide partners.

Through partnerships with leading carriers, CMS is able to guarantee sea freight capacities and easily adapt to growing trade Volumes - a key benefit when it comes to managing global supply chains.

Our more than 7,500 sea freight specialists can provide flexible solutions customized to meet your unique requirements which include:

  • Forest products
  • Reefer/perishables
  • Oil and energy
  • Automotive
  • Customs clearance/brokerage
  • Full Container Loaded (FCL): 20’, 40’, 40’HC
  • Less Container Loaded (LCL) with Consolidation System
  • Land transport (trailer truck delivery of container)
